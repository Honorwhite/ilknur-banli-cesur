Circumcision & Urology

Modern Circumcision and Urology in Children

Circumcision is not just a religious or social tradition, but also a medical surgical procedure. In Adana, Assoc. Prof. İlknur Banlı Cesur performs the circumcision procedure within an operating room discipline, focusing on minimum pain and the best aesthetic results. Circumcision performed by a pediatric surgeon protects the child's future urological health while minimizing potential risks.

In our modern understanding of circumcision, the child's psychological state is at least as important as the medical procedure. Painless methods are preferred, such as circumcisions performed with local anesthesia in infants and with short-term sedation (sleep) in older children. With the detailed urological examination performed before circumcision, additional problems such as undescended testis or hypospadias can also be detected.

Our Circumcision Methods and Urological Approach

The circumcision and urological follow-up processes we apply in our clinic are as follows:

  • Newborn Circumcision (First 28 days)
  • Infant Circumcision with Local Anesthesia
  • Painless Child Circumcision Under Sedation
  • Plastic Surgery Techniques with Aesthetic Sutures
  • Correction of Circumcision Errors (Revision)
  • Buried Penis Repair

Why Shoud a Pediatric Surgeon Perform It?

Although circumcision seems like a simple procedure, it requires expertise.

  • They are the expert who knows the urological anatomy best
  • Bleeding and infection risks are minimized
  • Problems such as curvature and adhesions are prevented
  • Psychological trauma is prevented

Post-Operative Care

The recovery process after modern circumcision is quite fast.

  • Usually return to normal life in 2-3 days
  • Methods without the trouble of removing stitches
  • Special painkiller protocols
  • 24/7 access to your doctor

For Healthy Men of the Future

Circumcision is one of the most important milestones in a man's life. It is our duty to conclude this memory in a non-traumatic, healthy, and aesthetic way. As Assoc. Prof. İlknur Banlı Cesur in Adana, we promise a safe and comfortable circumcision process to our families with our experience of thousands of successful circumcisions.

Medically, circumcision can be performed at any age, but the most ideal periods are the newborn period (first month) or before 2 years of age and after 6 years of age. Between the ages of 2-6, it is a period when it is recommended to postpone circumcision unless there is a medical necessity due to the psychosexual development of children.

Local anesthesia is quite comfortable for newborns and infants in the first 3-6 months. However, circumcision performed with short-term sedation (mild sleep) in older children who are active and fearful is much superior for both surgical meticulousness and the child's psychology.
