About Me

Biography

Assoc. Prof. İlknur Banlı Cesur

Born in Adana in 1978, Assoc. Prof. İlknur Banlı Cesur offers healing in a wide range from the newborn period to adolescence with her 20 years of experience in the field of pediatric surgery.

Throughout her professional life, she has aimed not only to treat diseases but also to improve the quality of life of children, inform families accurately and clearly, and offer a safe treatment process. Her scientific studies and academic journey are an integral part of her medical practice.

Career Journey

Education and Professional Background

1978

Birth and High School Education

Born in Adana. Successfully completed high school education at Adana Girls' High School.

1997 - 2004

Erciyes University Faculty of Medicine

Entered Kayseri Erciyes University Faculty of Medicine and graduated in 2004.

2005 - 2011

Çukurova University (Specialization)

Completed specialty training in the Department of Pediatric Surgery, Çukurova University.

2011 - 2017

Adana Numune T.R.H.

Continued her work within Adana Numune Training and Research Hospital.

2017 - 2023

Adana City Hospital

Contributed to the establishment of the Pediatric Surgery Department of UHS Adana City Hospital.

2023 - 2025

Associate Professor Title

Obtained the title of Associate Professor in 2023 and worked as an Associate Professor Doctor within UHS.

2025 - Present

Special Adana Ortadoğu Hospital

Continuing to accept patients within Special Adana Ortadoğu Hospital as of May 2025.
