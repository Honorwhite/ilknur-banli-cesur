Skin & Soft Tissue Surgery

Skin & Soft Tissue Lesions in Children

Masses on the skin, moles, or swellings under the soft tissue are common in children. These lesions should be treated correctly both functionally and aesthetically. Assoc. Prof. İlknur Banlı Cesur performs these treatments in accordance with surgical principles and by considering aesthetic concerns.

Main Surgeries Performed

Procedures we perform within the scope of skin and soft tissue:

  • Lipoma excision
  • Epidermal cyst and dermoid cyst surgery
  • Lymphangioma surgery
  • Soft tissue masses
  • Pilonidal sinus (ingrown hair) surgery
  • Surgical interventions for hemangiomas or selected vascular anomalies

Our main goal in these surgeries is to obtain permanent and healthy results by using aesthetic stitching techniques that will leave minimal scars.
