About Me

20 Years of Experience and Academic Background

After graduating from Adana Girls' High School and Erciyes University Faculty of Medicine, I completed my specialization training at Çukurova University. I served in the establishment of the Department of Pediatric Surgery at Adana City Hospital and received the title of Associate Professor in 2023. Currently, I am serving at Special Adana Ortadoğu Hospital.
